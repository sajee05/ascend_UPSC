Q1) Consider the following statements:
1.  The Parliamentary Forum on Population and Public Health is the first Parliamentary Forum.
2.  The first Parliamentary Forum was established in 2004.

b)  Only 2
c)  Both 1 and 2
d)  Neither 1 nor 2
