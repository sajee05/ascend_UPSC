Q1) Consider the following statements:
1.  The district judge exercises both judicial and administrative powers.
2.  The sessions judge has no power to impose capital punishment.
Which of the statements given above is / are correct?
a)  Only 1
b)  Only 2
c)  Both 1 and 2
d)  Neither 1 nor 2
#QuestionEnd
#AnswerStart
Answer: a)  Only 1

Q5) Which one of the following is not a qualification for a person to be appointed as district judge?
