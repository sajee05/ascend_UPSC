Q1) Consider the following statements:
1.  The non-cabinet ministers are debarred from the membership of the Cabinet Committees.
2.  The Cabinet Committees are an organizational device to reduce the enormous workload of the cabinet.
Which of the statements given above is/are correct ?
a)  Only 1
b)  Only 2
c)  Both 1 and 2
d)  Neither 1 nor 2
#QuestionEnd
#AnswerStart
Answer: b)  Only 2
#AnswerEnd

a)  Constitution of India
b)  Parliamentary Statutes
c)  Rules of Business
d)  Presidential Orders
#QuestionEnd
#AnswerStart
