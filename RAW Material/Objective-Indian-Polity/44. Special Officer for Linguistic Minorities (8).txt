Q1) Which of the following committees / commissions recommended the establishment of the office of the Special Officer for Linguistic Minorities?
a)  Administrative Reforms Commission
b)  Swaran Singh Committee
c)  States Reorganisation Commission
d)  Rajamannar Committee
#QuestionEnd
#AnswerStart
Answer: c)  States Reorganisation Commission
#AnswerEnd

Q2) The Commissioner for Linguistic Minorities submits his annual reports and other reports to the:
a)  National Commission for Minorities
b)  Parliament of India
c)  President of India
d)  Union Cabinet
#QuestionEnd

Answer: c)  President of India
