Q1) In a given year in India, official poverty lines are higher in some States than in others because: (2019)
a) poverty rates vary from State to State.
b) price levels vary from State to State.
c) Gross State Product varies from State to State.
d) quality of public distribution varies from State.
#QuestionEnd
#AnswerStart
Answer: b) price levels vary from State to State.
#AnswerEnd
